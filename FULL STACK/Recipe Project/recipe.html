Gulaab Jamun

Ingridients

For Dough :

  1. Khoya (Mawa) - 1 cup (grated)
  2. Maida (All-purpose flour) - 2 tablespoons
  3. Baking powder - 1/4 teaspoon
  4. Milk - 1-2 tablespoons (as needed to make a smooth dough)
  5. Cardamom powder - 1/4 teaspoon (optional)

    For the Sugar Syrup :

  1. Sugar - 2 cups
  2. Water - 1.5 cups
  3. Cardamom pods - 4-5 (slightly crushed)
  4. Rose water - 1 teaspoon (optional)
  5. Saffron strands - A few (optional for added flavor and aroma)

    For Frying:

  1. Ghee or Oil - For deep frying

Recipie :

  1. Make Sugar Syrup:
    • Combine sugar and water, boil until slightly sticky (no string consistency needed).
    • Add cardamom powder and rose water. Keep warm.
  2. Prepare Dough:
    • Mix milk powder, flour, and baking soda. Add ghee and mix until crumbly.
    • Gradually add milk to form a soft, smooth dough. Rest for 10 minutes.
  3. Shape Gulab Jamun:
    • Divide dough into small balls, ensuring they are smooth and crack-free.
  4. Fry the Balls:
    • Heat ghee or oil on low-medium heat. Fry the balls until golden brown, stirring gently for even color.
  5. Soak in Syrup:
    • Add fried gulab jamun to warm sugar syrup. Let them soak for at least 2 hours.

Rasugulla

Ingridients

    For the Rasgulla:

  1. Milk - 1 liter (preferably full cream or whole milk)
  2. Lemon Juice/Vinegar - 2-3 tablespoons (to curdle the milk)
  3. Water - For kneading and boiling
  4. Ice Cubes - A few (to cool the curdled milk and make soft chhena)

    For the Sugar Syrup :

  1. Sugar - 2 cups
  2. Water - 4 cups
  3. Cardamom pods - 1/4 teaspoon (slightly crushed)
  4. Rose water - 1 teaspoon (optional)

Recipe :

  1. Make Sugar Syrup:
    • Boil sugar and water until slightly sticky. Add cardamom and rose water.
  2. Prepare Dough:
    • Mix gulab jamun mix with milk to form a smooth, soft dough. Rest for 5 minutes.
  3. Shape and Fry:
    • Shape into small crack-free balls. Fry on low-medium heat until golden brown.
  4. Soak:
    • Add fried balls to warm sugar syrup and let soak for 1-2 hours.
